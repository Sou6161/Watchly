# Delete your Watchly account

Google Play requires a way to request account deletion **without reinstalling the
app**, which is what this page is for. If you still have Watchly installed, the
in-app route is faster and instant.

## In the app (instant)

1. Open Watchly
2. **Profile** (top right)
3. Scroll down → **Delete my account**
4. Enter your password to confirm

It happens immediately and cannot be undone.

## Without the app

Email **sourabh61saini@gmail.com** from the address you signed up with, subject
line *Delete my account*.

We'll confirm and delete within **30 days**, usually much sooner. We'll only ask
you to prove you own the address — never for your password.

## What gets deleted

- Your account: email, password hash, display name, region, streaming services
- Every session you started
- Every swipe you made in those sessions
- Your saved partner, if you had one
- Your analytics events

## What is kept, and why

If you played on **separate phones** with someone else, that session is part of
**their** history as well as yours. Deleting your account doesn't erase their
record of the night you watched something together.

What happens instead: your account is unlinked from it, and only the display name
you were using at the time remains, as plain text, so their history still reads
"2 matches with Sourabh" rather than "2 matches with null". Nothing else of yours
survives — not your email, not your swipes, not your account.

Cached film and series information from TMDB is shared reference data that was
never yours, and stays.
